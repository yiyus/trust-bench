Every figure matches the commit's methodology.md text and PR doc exactly.

### Note: the correction to #95's existing text is warranted and precise

#95's original "Status classification" paragraph attributed the `ill_conditioning`(kappa)/`dimensionality`(n) STALLED-reclassification story to Hessian indefiniteness in both, and the old "out of scope" note deferred a decision assuming ill_conditioning and the typical study were the same mechanism. Both are now corrected: `ill_conditioning`'s fragility is reattributed to numerical precision loss under extreme conditioning (matching the eigenvalue evidence above), and `dimensionality` is dropped from that paragraph since its own `trust-exact` never actually stalls (confirmed: `dimensionality` converges through `n=1000` per the still-standing BFGS section, and #95's own PR doc never claimed a `dimensionality` STALLED case - only `ill_conditioning`'s did). This is a real correction to previously-merged documentation, not a stylistic rewrite.

Rather than the "same mechanism" claim #110's own acceptance criteria assumed, the new section frames `dimensionality` (mild, harmless indefiniteness) and the typical study (severe, fatal indefiniteness) as the same mechanism at different severities, while explicitly calling out that `ill_conditioning`'s superficially similar symptom is a different mechanism and "should not be read as the same boundary."
